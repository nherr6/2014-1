2014

2014 is a feeling. That feeling when you were 11 years old, sitting on the couch watching the big game. You hear your mother in the kitchen, shuffling about as the smell of pizza bites fills your nostrils. She brings them to you in a tray with ranch dip on the side.

"I love you," she says, ruffling your hair.

You can't say it then, but you feel it in your chest. the words, I love you too, echo within you.
